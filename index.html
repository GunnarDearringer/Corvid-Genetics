Corvid Genetics
About Logs Conclusion

What if crows evolved intelligence like ours? Would they create gods, myths, rituals? Would we finally see the roots of belief?

The Experiment

Crows are already among the smartest animals on Earth — capable of memory, communication, and basic tools. Yet they never built societies. In this thought experiment, we imagine artificially accelerating crow evolution to observe how intelligence creates culture, belief systems, and possibly... religion.

Research Logs

Day 30 – Symbolic Burials

Crows begin burying shiny objects in specific locations. Early signs of ritual behavior emerge.

Day 100 – Totemic Behavior

Groups start gathering around specific objects. One group is observed 'dancing' during storms. Are they interpreting weather as supernatural?

Day 300 – Emergence of Sky Deity

A story is repeatedly mimed by older crows: a giant bird in the sky brings light and death. Religion... has begun.

Final Thoughts

As we observe crow society evolve, a key question emerges: is religion a natural byproduct of intelligence, or a unique artifact of human consciousness? If crows create belief systems, what does that say about our own?
