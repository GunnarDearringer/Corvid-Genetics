Corvid Genetics
About Logs Conclusion

What if crows evolved intelligence like ours? Would they create gods, myths, rituals? Would we finally see the roots of belief?

The Experiment

Crows are among the smartest animals — capable of memory, communication, and basic tools. Yet they never built societies. What would happen if we accelerated their evolution?

Research Logs

Day 30 – Symbolic Burials

Crows begin burying shiny objects. Early signs of ritual behavior emerge.

Day 100 – Totemic Behavior

Groups gather around specific objects, mimicking worship behaviors during storms.

Day 300 – Emergence of Sky Deity

Older crows repeatedly act out a sky god story — religion... has begun.

Final Thoughts

If crows can create gods, myths, and society when given the spark of intelligence, what does it reveal about humanity’s origins?
